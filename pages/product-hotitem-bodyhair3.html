QUICK HAIR PUFF

A powder-type hair puff controlling your hair line with natural color

• Try the quick hair puff to cover an empty hair line giving your hair body and making your facial line look sharp.
• Strengthen adhesion of powder to the scalp with the puff
• Natural brown/dark brown colors similar to natural hair color to produce a natural hair line cover and give body to your hair
• Long-lasting waterproof product

DIRECTIONS

Remove the cap inside the container, and gently tap on the thinning or receding hairline with the puff.
WARNING: Do not twist open after removing the inner cap.

CAUTION

1. Wash off immediately if powder gets into the eyes.
2. Discontinue use if irritation or rash appears.
3. Store the container in an upright position
4. Be sure to protect clothing, bath, and bed linens during use.
