MULTI-STICK CONTOUR

The Faceshop multi-stick producing a desired makeup effect with a single touch

• CONTOUR - The easy-to-use contour stick comes in a natural-looking neutral shade that goes on smooth and feels weightless on skin. It helps add dimension and re ne your features.
• HIGHLIGHTER - The fine pearl particles reflect light, adding a luxurious shimmer to makeup.
• PORE-SMOOTHING - This pore-smoothing stick helps minimize the appearance of pores and mattifies shine for longer-lasting makeup.

DIRECTIONS

CONTOUR
1) For the most seamless, natural-looking contouring, apply the contour stick before foundation (or BB or CC cream). Pat a small amount of foundation over the contour shade using a sponge or your fingers.
2) For a more sculpted look, apply the contour stick after foundation to the sides of the nose and the jawline and under the cheekbones. Gently pat the edges with your fingers to blend out.
HIGHLIGHTER
1) For the most subtle and luxurious glow, apply highlighter before foundation (or BB or CC cream). Pat a small amount of foundation over highlighted area using a sponge or your fingers.
2) To further accentuate your features, apply highlighter after foundation to the bridge of the nose, the centre of the forehead, the top of the chin and the brow bones. Gently pat the edges with your fingers to blend out.

CAUTION

For external use only. Keep out of the reach of children. Discontinue use if irritation or rash occurs. Replace cap after using. Do not store at extreme temperatures or in direct sunlight.
